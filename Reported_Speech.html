to reportÂ (verb): to tell somebody what you have heard or seen

If we want to say what somebody has said, we basically have two

  1. We can use the person's exact words - in quotation marks "..." if we are writing (direct speech).
  1. We can change the person's words into our own words (reported speech).

direct speech

(exact words)

He said: "I love you."

reported speech

(my words)

He said that he loved me.
