Writing Checklist for English Learners

Planning and Content

✔ Did I think about or brainstorm my topic before writing?

✔ Can I picture my target reader(s)?

✔ Did I plan my topic and important details?

✔ Did I think of interesting examples?

✔ Did I find a way to hook my reader at the beginning?

✔ Do I know enough about the topic?

Vocabulary

✔ Did I write in simple English?

✔ Do I know what all of the words mean?

✔ Did I start my sentences with capital letters?

✔ Did I use capital letters for proper nouns?

✔ Did I use phrasal verbs or idioms properly?

Organization

✔ Did I break my writing up with paragraphs?

✔ Did I use topic sentences?

✔ Did I use transitional phrases?

✔ Are my sentences in the right sequence?

✔ Is there a beginning, middle and end?

Proofing and Final Stage

✔ Did I check my spelling?

✔ Did I use punctuation correctly? (commas especially)

✔ Did I give my writing time to breathe?

✔ Did I proofread before publishing or submitting?

✔ Did I cut out superfluous sentences or words?

✔ Did I use proper citation if necessary?

✔ Did I ask another person to proofread my important writing (formal assignment, application or resumé)?

✔ Did I read my writing out loud to myself?

✔ Will my reader have something to say about this?
